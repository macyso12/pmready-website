About the Program

Explore the world of tech before you choose your path

The TCE Fellowship is designed to give high school students a real look inside the tech industry. Through sessions with industry professionals, hands-on projects, and a final Demo Day, participants walk away with practical skills and the clarity to make informed decisions about their future careers.

The program covers three disciplines Software Engineering, UX Design, and Product Management so students can discover where their strengths and interests truly lie.

💻
Software Engineering Key concepts, tools, and the development lifecycle
🎨
User Experience (UX) Design thinking, user research, and prototyping
📦
Product Management Roadmapping, prioritization, and product strategy
Learning Goals

What you'll gain from the TCE Fellowship

🔭

Holistic Understanding

Gain deep insights into the complete processes of software development, UX design, and product management not just the surface level.

🛠️

Real-World Application

Work on hands-on projects and interact directly with industry professionals to build practical, resume-worthy experience.

🎯

Informed Career Decisions

Leave the program better prepared for higher education and a career in tech with the clarity to choose a path that fits you.
